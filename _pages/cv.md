## 🏢 About the Lab

The **Intelligent Decision and Control Laboratory (IDCL)** is affiliated with the School of Control Science and Engineering, Dalian University of Technology.

The lab focuses on intelligent decision-making, distributed control, multi-agent systems, and autonomous robotic systems operating under communication constraints and uncertain environments.

Our mission is to integrate theoretical advances in control, optimization, and cooperative intelligence with real-world robotic platforms and field experiments.

---

## 🔬 Research Groups

### 1️⃣ Robotic Manipulation Group

This group focuses on:

- Autonomous robotic manipulation  
- Vision-guided control and perception  
- Learning-based motion planning  
- Multi-arm collaborative coordination

### 2️⃣ Multi-UAV Cooperative Search Group

This group focuses on:

- Cooperative decision-making for multi-UAV systems in uncertain environments  
- Autonomous coordination and large-scale area exploration  
- Distributed collaboration under communication and resource constraints  
- Robust collective behavior in dynamic and partially observable scenarios
